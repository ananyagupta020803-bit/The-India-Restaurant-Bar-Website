The India Restaurant & Bar Pokeno

The India Restaurant & Bar offers the best out of Indian cuisine. Our restaurants are located in Tuakau, Pokeno, and Pukekohe in the Franklin Area.

Our menu offers a wide range of authentic Indian vegetarian and non-vegetarian food such as Butter Chicken, Chicken Tikka Masala, Chilli Chicken, Dal Makhani, and many more. We cook fresh entrées on slow charcoal to ensure rich and authentic flavors. The India Restaurant is the perfect relaxing place for a quick lunch, takeaway, or a romantic dinner.
